Honest transparency

Blockchain can enable more transparent and accurate end-to-end tracking in the supply chain: We digitized physical assets and created a decentralized immutable record of all transactions, making it possible to track assets from production to delivery or use by end user. Improving traceability also adds value by mitigating the high costs of quality problems, such as recalls, reputational damage, or the loss of revenue from black- or grey-market products.

Streamlined operations

Blockchain can streamline administrative processes and reduce costs by enabling an effective audit of supply chain data. Processes involving manual checks for compliance or credit purposes that may currently take weeks can be accelerated through a distributed ledger of all relevant information
